Pelmeni Recipe

Description

Pelmeni are Russian meat-filled dumplings.

These dumplings are a common convenience food. Large batches can be frozen and quickly boiled or fried on demand.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 Large egg
  • 1 Teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 Cup warm water, or more as needed
  • 3 Cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tablespoon all-purpose flour for dusting

Filling

  • 18 Ounces ground beef
  • 1 Small onion, chopped
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons ice-cold water
  • 1 Tablespoon coarse salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. To make dough: Combine egg, oil, and salt in a liquid measuring cup.
  2. Add enough warm water to fill to 1 cup.
  3. Pour into a large bowl and knead in 3 cups flour until smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover with a dish towel and let dough rest for 30 minutes.
  5. While dough is resting, make filling:
  6. Combine ground beef, onion, water, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl
  7. Mix thoroughly by hand or using a fork. Set aside.
  8. Dust a baking sheet lightly with 1 tablespoon flour; set aside.
  9. Roll out a portion of dough very thinly on a lightly floured surface
  10. Keep remaining dough covered with a dish towel to avoid drying out
  11. Cut out 2 1/2-inch rounds with a cookie cutter.
  12. Place about 1 teaspoon filling on one side of each dough circle. Fold dough over and seal the edges using your fingers, forming a crescent.
  13. Join the ends and pinch them together.
  14. Place on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat with remaining dough and filling.
  15. Freeze pelmeni for 30 minutes to prevent them from sticking together.
  16. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  17. Working in batches, cook frozen pelmeni until they float to the top, about 5 minutes.
  18. Continue cooking for 3-5 more minutes. Transfer to serving plates using a slotted spoon.
