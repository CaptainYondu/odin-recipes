Gyudon(Beef Rice Bowl) Recipe

Description

Gyudon is one of the most popular rice bowl dishes in Japan.

Simmered beef is served on top of steamed rice. Topped with beni shoga (pickled red ginger)

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups cooked rice
  • 6 Tablespoons mirin (Japanese sweet wine)
  • 3 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons sake
  • 1 Onion, cut into wedges
  • 10 Ounces beef, thinly sliced
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon pickled ginger(beni shoga)

Steps

  1. Divide rice evenly between 4 donburi bowls.
  2. Bring mirin, soy sauce, and sake to a boil in a pot over medium heat.
  3. Add onion; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in beef; simmer until no longer pink and cooked through, about 5 minutes.
  5. Spoon beef and onion over rice using a slotted spoon.
  6. Place eggs in the pot and bring to a boil.
  7. Cook until soft boiled, 5 to 7 minutes.
  8. Peel and add to bowls.
  9. Garnish with pickled ginger.
